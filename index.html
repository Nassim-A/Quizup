What is QuizUp ?

QuizUp originally was a discontinued mobile game developed and published by Iceland-based Plain Vanilla Games, and later operated by Glu Mobile. The game was a mobile trivia app similar to the game Trivial Pursuit. QuizUp was a multiplayer game in which one user competes against another during seven rounds of timed multiple-choice questions of various topics. There were over 1,200 total topics available to users to choose from, and all the questions were voluntarily submitted by content contributors. Most topics were available in several different languages.

How to play ?

First of all, you need to create an account by clicking on the sign up button on the top right corner of the page. Once that is done, you will be able to choose a theme/category of questions and choose the game mode you wish to play : Solo / Online. A solo game consists of two mode : Training(Untimed) and Timed. While an online game will only consist of a timed game against other players or friends.

How is the game score calculated ?

      - Each game consists of 10 questions.
      - The player with the highest score wins.
      - Each question has a timer of 20 seconds.
      - Wrong answer : 0 points.
      - Right answer : 10 points × (Time left ÷ 10).
      - For example : If a player has found the right answer within 12 seconds, his current score would be : 8 points (10 points × 0.8).
      - The score of the game is the sum of the questions' scores.
